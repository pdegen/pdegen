## Web Development

### QuoteBrowser

One reason why I prefer to read e-books is that I can easily highlight noteworthy passages and quickly retrieve them across my entire digital library. At time of writing, I have close to 5'000 highlights (aka clippings) saved on my Kindle, which I frequently consult whenever I'm writing about a topic on which I have read something before. However, these clippings are stored in a plain text file, which is unwieldy to navigate. Therefore, I made a simple (static) web app that lets users upload their clippings for improved browsing, sorting, filtering, and sharing.

[https://github.com/pdegen/QuoteBrowser](https://github.com/pdegen/QuoteBrowser)
